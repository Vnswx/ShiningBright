Shining Bright is a Jakarta-based brand founded by Mr. Ryan Sastro and Mr. Tara Budiman in 2012. Starting as one of Jakarta’s streetwear clothing lines, the brand has grown into a widely recognized and influential name over the years. We focus on creating daily essentials, including apparel, footwear, household items, and accessories. Today, Shining Bright has numerous resellers both nationwide and internationally, and our products are available through our official website, WhatsApp, marketplaces, and online shopping applications.

Known for its impactful collaborations, Shining Bright has partnered with various collectives, movements, and brands, including Warner Bros., as well as internationally acclaimed local artists such as Chuck, Harsya, Heri Mardika, and Agan Harahap. With the core values of Fashion, Art, Community, and Popular Culture, we are committed to delivering the best experiences for our customers. Interacting with them is not only our pleasure but also an essential part of who we are as a brand.
